## Adsloty - Newsletter Advertising Marketplace

Adsloty is a self-service newsletter advertising platform that connects newsletter writers/creators with sponsors (advertisers) who want to place ads in newsletters.

### The Problem It Solves

**For Newsletter Writers:**
- Monetizing newsletters is hard - manually negotiating with sponsors is time-consuming
- Managing ad slot availability, pricing, and bookings requires significant effort
- Chasing payments and handling refunds is a hassle

**For Sponsors/Advertisers:**
- Finding newsletters that match their target audience is difficult
- The traditional process involves back-and-forth emails with writers
- No standardized way to book, pay, and track ad placements
